Founder

A Surgeon Who Built a Movement

dr. Rizal Irvan Shiddieqy Pohan, SpOT (K)-Spine

Founder & President Director — AMPI & ACM

Dr. Rizal Irvan Shiddieqy Pohan is a distinguished orthopaedic surgeon and spine specialist with over 30 years of experience in Indonesia's healthcare landscape. Renowned for his clinical expertise and leadership, he has been a transformative force in advancing orthopaedic care nationwide.

He served as Past President of PABOI (2012–2014), is an active Spine Consultant, and mentors the next generation of spine surgeons across Indonesia. As a National Board Examiner for PABOI, Dr. Rizal upholds the highest standards in medical education and practice.

Dr. Rizal practices at RS Mayapada and RS Bethsaida, known for his patient-centered approach and innovative surgical techniques. He founded PMN Company in 2013, then established the Adhira group in 2018 — driven by a vision to democratize access to high-quality orthopaedic implants throughout Indonesia.

Today, the Adhira group serves 181+ hospitals, supporting over 450 surgical procedures monthly — a direct reflection of his lifelong mission to improve patient outcomes nationwide.

Vision & Mission

What We Stand For

Our Vision

To comprehensively provide the needs for implants and medical instruments, especially for orthopaedic cases, throughout Indonesia — ensuring that every region has access to high-quality, complete, and reliable orthopaedic medical solutions, supporting the advancement of healthcare and improving patient outcomes nationwide.

Visi: Menyediakan Kebutuhan Implant dan instrument medis khususnya pada kasus Ortopedi di Seluruh Indonesia secara Paripurna.

Our Mission

  1. Provide comprehensive services with the latest technology in orthopaedic implant products.
  2. Support education and training for doctors and nurses to ensure patient safety and the comfort of medical personnel.
  3. Maintain a strong commitment to the services required by hospitals and doctors, especially by ensuring the availability of implants.
  4. Offer high-quality implants at affordable prices, enabling doctors to serve patients in all types of hospitals.
